How to delete menu items

Delete entire sections or individual items from your menu.

Overview

There are two ways to remove content from your menu: delete entire sections, or delete individual items.

1) Delete an entire section

  1. Open the Menu screen in the Partner app.
  2. Tap the hamburger button at the top‑right to enter edit mode.
  3. In edit mode, each section shows a trash icon. Tap the trash icon next to a section to delete it and all of its items.
Tap the top‑right button to enter edit mode.
In edit mode, tap the trash icon to delete a section.

2) Delete an individual item

  1. On the Menu screen, tap the three‑dot menu on the item.
  2. Choose Delete from the action sheet to remove just that item.
Use the item menu to delete a single item.
