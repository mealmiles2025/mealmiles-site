Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-08-13

What We Collect

  • Account info: name, email (for receipts/support).
  • Order details and preferences.
  • Payment tokens and related metadata (processed by Stripe — MealMiles does not store full card numbers).
  • Device and usage data for performance, security, and fraud prevention (e.g., Firebase App Check).

How We Use Data

  • Provide and improve ordering, loyalty, and customer support.
  • Prevent fraud and ensure platform security.
  • Comply with law and enforce our Terms.

Sharing

We share necessary order and contact information with Restaurants to fulfill your order. We also share data with processors such as Stripe (payments), Firebase (infrastructure), and analytics providers under appropriate agreements.

Your Choices

  • To access, correct, or delete your information, use the contact form on our website.
  • You can opt out of marketing emails using the link included in those emails.

Security

We use industry-standard safeguards including TLS, Stripe for payment processing, and Firebase App Check. No method is 100% secure, but we work to protect your data.
