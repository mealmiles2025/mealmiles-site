MealMiles (“MealMiles”, “we”, “us”) provides a consumer mobile app that lets you discover restaurants, place orders, and earn loyalty rewards. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use the Consumer app, our websites, and related services.

Information We Collect

  • Account info: name, email, and password hash.
  • Order & loyalty data: items, totals, timestamps, points earned/redeemed.
  • Payment: tokens and limited metadata handled by Stripe. We never store full card numbers.
  • Communications: support messages and notification preferences (push/email).
  • Device & usage: app version, device identifiers, and diagnostic logs for performance and fraud prevention.
  • Location (optional): if you grant permission (e.g., to show nearby restaurants). You can revoke this in your OS settings.

How We Use Information

  • Provide ordering, rewards, receipts, and customer support.
  • Process payments via Stripe and maintain transaction history.
  • Personalize content (e.g., relevant offers) and improve app performance.
  • Send service messages (order status, receipts). Marketing messages are sent only in line with your preferences and applicable law.
  • Prevent fraud, secure our services, and comply with legal obligations.

How We Share

We share information with service providers (e.g., hosting, analytics, Stripe) under contracts limiting their use to providing services to us. Order-level details relevant to fulfilling your order are shared with the restaurant you order from. We may disclose information to comply with law or protect rights and safety, and in connection with a business transaction (e.g., merger).

Your Choices

  • Update some profile info in the app; for other requests contact support@getmealmiles.com.
  • Manage push/email settings in Profile → Notification Preferences.
  • Control device permissions (location, notifications) in your phone settings.
  • Unsubscribe links are included in marketing emails.

Region-Specific Rights

Depending on where you live (e.g., California, certain Canadian provinces, EU/UK), you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or opt out of certain processing. Contact us using the email below and we’ll help exercise your rights as applicable.

Retention

We retain information as needed to provide the service, comply with law, and resolve disputes. When no longer required, we delete or de-identify it.

Security

We use administrative, technical, and physical safeguards appropriate to the nature of the data. No system is 100% secure.

Children

The app is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your region). We do not knowingly collect such data.

Contact

Questions or requests? Email support@getmealmiles.com.
