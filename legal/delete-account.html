Delete Your Account

This page explains how to delete your MealMiles account and request deletion of associated data. You can delete directly inside the app or submit a web request below.

Delete Your Account in the App

Consumer app (MealMiles)

  1. Open the MealMiles app and sign in.
  2. Go to Profile → Account.
  3. Tap Delete Account and confirm.

Partner app (MealMiles Partner)

  1. Open the Partner app and sign in.
  2. Go to Account → Settings.
  3. Tap Delete Account and confirm.

We may ask you to re-authenticate before deletion. Once submitted, deletion is queued and processed as described below.

Request Deletion Without the App

If you no longer have the app installed, you can request deletion using our support form. Please include the email and phone number associated with your MealMiles account so we can verify ownership.

Open Support Form

If you prefer email, contact us via the form and we’ll reply from our support address with verification steps.

What We Delete vs. Retain

Deleted

  • Account profile (name, email, phone) and authentication identifiers.
  • Consumer loyalty profile (MealMiles points balance) and non-financial user preferences.
  • Device tokens used for push notifications.

Retained (legal or operational necessity)

  • Transactional/financial records and receipts processed by Stripe (and by restaurants as merchants of record) required for accounting, audit, tax, fraud prevention, and chargeback purposes.
  • Order records may be retained or anonymized as required by law and/or restaurant obligations (e.g., food safety, reconciliation).
  • Logs needed to demonstrate security, consent, and compliance may be retained for a limited period.

Retention is based on applicable laws and our legitimate interests (e.g., fraud prevention and financial compliance). Retained data is restricted and not used for marketing after deletion.

Timeline & Verification

  • Verification: We may ask you to confirm the request via a code or reply from the registered email/phone.
  • Processing time: Most requests complete within 7 days; complex financial/order reconciliations may take up to 30 days.
  • Subscriptions: MealMiles does not sell subscriptions; deleting your account does not cancel past charges with restaurants.
