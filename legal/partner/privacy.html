Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-08-13

MealMiles (“we”, “our”, “us”) provides a loyalty and ordering platform for restaurants (“Partners”) and their customers. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our apps, websites, and services.

What We Collect

  • Account information such as name, email, phone (for receipts and support).
  • Order details and loyalty activity.
  • Payment tokens & metadata processed by Stripe. We do not store full card numbers.
  • Device/usage data for performance, security, and fraud prevention.
  • Restaurant profile content (menus, photos, hours) for Partner accounts.

How We Use Data

  • Provide and improve ordering, loyalty, and customer support.
  • Facilitate payments and payouts via Stripe.
  • Prevent fraud and ensure platform security.
  • Comply with law and enforce our Terms.

Sharing

We share data with service providers (e.g., Stripe, analytics, hosting) under contracts that limit their use to providing services to us. We may disclose information as required by law or to protect rights and safety.

Your Choices

Access or update some information in the app or contact support@getmealmiles.com.
